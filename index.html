Our Process

Step 1 Meet the Designer

The client meets with a designer to discuss their ideas and preferences, shaping the initial vision into a tangible plan.

Step 2 Site Visit and Measurements

The company conducts a site visit to assess the project's feasibility, take measurements, and brainstorm ideas with the client.

Step 3 Comprehensive Quotation and Analysis

After the site visit, the company provides a detailed quotation outlining the project scope and costs involved, emphasizing transparency and accuracy.

Step 4 Designing and Calculation.

Upon approval of the quotation, the company begins the design process, meticulously considering every detail to exceed the client's expectations while adhering to budget and schedule.

Step 5 Payment

Once the design is finalized, the company discusses payment options and schedules, aiming to make the process simple and straightforward for the client.

Step 6 Material Selection

The client is assisted in selecting materials for the project, with the company offering guidance and expertise to ensure the perfect choice in terms of quality, finishes, and textures.
